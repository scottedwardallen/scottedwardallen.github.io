# Scott E. Allen

Researcher working at the intersection of neuroscience, physics, trust, learning, and implementation.

<div style="display:flex; gap:40px; align-items:center; margin-top:30px; flex-wrap:wrap;">

<div style="flex:1; min-width:300px;">

I study how distinct neural learning systems, operating over different information structures, shape human behavior. My work connects neural representations to education, trust, and real-world implementation.

I am especially interested in building mechanistic bridges from neural systems to cognition, social behavior, and instructional practice.
